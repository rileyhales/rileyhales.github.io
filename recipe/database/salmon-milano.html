Salmon Milano

  • this is the salmon at Costco in the prepared food section

    • Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of salmon
  • Sprig of dill to add to the pan (optional)
  • Basil Pesto Butter:
  • ¼ cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, softened

    • Instructions

    Blend in a food processor or blender until smooth. Place one scoop of butter (about 2 tablespoons) on a piece of salmon. Bake at 400 degrees in an ungreased baking pan with a sprig of dill on the side (optional). Bake for 25-30 minutes or until 145 degrees.

    *You can use store-bought pesto as well. Just add some (maybe 2 Tablespoons or whatever looks good) to ½ cup of softened butter.

    *You can also cook it in the instant pot. I put a piece of foil on the rack after adding 1 cup of water to the bottom and put the fish and butter pesto on top of the foil. Steam on high pressure for 3 minutes. I can only fit 2 pieces of salmon on the foil.
