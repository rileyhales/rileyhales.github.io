Crustless Broccoli Cheese Quiche

Ingredients

  • 3 cups chopped broccoli florets
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • ⅔ cup of milk
  • ¼ cup half and half
  • 5 large eggs
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon pepper
  • Pinch of nutmeg

    • Instructions

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Spray a pie dish with oil (Pam). Steam chopped broccoli in the microwave with 1 tablespoon water until tender crisp and green, but not mushy, about 2 ½-3 minutes. Evenly spread the broccoli in the dish and top it evenly with grated cheddar cheese.

    Make the custard by whisking together the milk, half and half, eggs, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Pour the custard over the broccoli and bake 35-40 minutes until the center is set.

    Yield: 6-8 servings
