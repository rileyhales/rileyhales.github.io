Kings Hawaiian Slider Sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 24 pack Kings Hawaiian rolls
  • Â½ lb. sliced ham
  • 12 slices swiss cheese
  • 6 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried onion
  • 1 teaspoon poppy seeds

    • Instructions

    Slice rolls and layer the bottom sections of the roll in a 9x13 pan. Spread ham over the rolls and then the cheese. Place the tops of the rolls on the top of the cheese layer. In a saucepan, melt the butter, worcestershire sauce, mustard and onion. Pour over the rolls. Sprinkle with poppy seeds. Cover with foil and refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight is best. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes covered with foil and 5 more minutes uncovered.
