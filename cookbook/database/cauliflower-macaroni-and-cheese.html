Cauliflower Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 8 cups of water
  • 2 cups chopped cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup uncooked elbow macaroni
  • ½ cup milk
  • 3 Tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup Italian style panko crispy bread crumbs
  • 1 Tablespoon melted butter

    • Instructions

    In a 4 quart saucepan, heat water to boiling. Add cauliflower and macaroni; cook 5-7 minutes stirring frequently until macaroni is tender. Drain and return to the saucepan. Cover to keep warm.

    Heat oven to 350 degrees and spray 2 quart baking dish with cooking spray. In a medium size saucepan, stir milk, flour, mustard, salt and pepper with a whisk until smooth. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils and thickens. Remove from heat and sti in cheese until melted.

    Add cheese sauce to cooked pasta mixture and stir well. Spoon into bakcin dish. In a small bowl, combine bread crumbs and melted butter. Sprinkle over pasta mixture in baking dish. Bake at 20-25 minutes or until the edges are bubbly.
