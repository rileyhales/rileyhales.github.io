Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded monterey jack cheese
  • 1 can evaporated milk

    • Instructions

    Lightly spray a 2 ½ quart casserole dish. Cook macaroni in a saucepan according to directions on box. Drain and add to saucepan. Add cheeses and milk, saving ½ cup of cheese to spread on the top. Bake 20 minutes covered with foil and then remove foil and bake another 10 minutes. Let sit for a few minutes before serving.

    *This recipe can be cut in half and also other cheeses can be used. I have used pepper jack in place of the monterey jack and it is very good. I have also added leftover crumbled bacon too.
