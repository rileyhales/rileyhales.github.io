Pizza Bombs

Ingredients

  • 1 can of biscuit dough (large “grand” size: use ½ biscuit)
  • ½ - 1 teaspoon pizza sauce per bomb
  • 1 cube mozzarella cheese per bomb

    • Instructions

    Spread biscuit into a 3-4 inch circle. Spread sauce on top and add 1 cube of cheese to center. Fold edges to the center and pinch to seal. Turn over and brush with a mixture of melted butter or margarine and italian seasoning. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Bake at 375 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
