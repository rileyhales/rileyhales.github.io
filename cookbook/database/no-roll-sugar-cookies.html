No-Roll Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • ¾ cup vegetable or canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 ¼ cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

    • Instructions

    In large bowl, beat 1 cup granulated sugar, powdered sugar, butter, oil, milk, vanilla and eggs with mixer. Stir in remaining ingredients except for ¼ cup granulated sugar. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours or until firm.

    Heat oven to 350 degrees. Place ¼ cup granulated sugar in small bowl and shape dough into balls (use cookie scoop to make them all the same size). Roll the balls in the sugar. Place on ungreased cookie sheet about 3 inches apart. Flatten to ¼-½ inch thickness with the bottom of a glass. Sprinkle cookies with a little additional sugar on top if needed.

    Bake about 12-13 minutes or until set and edges are just beginning to brown. Immediately remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack.
