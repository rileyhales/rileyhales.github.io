Six Sister’s Skillet Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound hamburger
  • 1 12 ounce package bow tie pasta
  • 1 jar favorite spaghetti sauce
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • ½ cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup sour cream

    • Instructions

    Brown beef and cook and drain noodles. Mix remaining ingredients together and put into a 13x9 baking dish. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
