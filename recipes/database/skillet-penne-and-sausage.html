Skillet Penne and Sausage

Ingredients

1 Tablespoon olive oil or canola oil

14-16 ounce fully cooked sausage (Hillshire farms polska kielbasa or other brand)

½ cup onion

1 - 12 package frozen veggies (I use california mix with carrots, cauliflower and broccoli)

2 cups chicken broth

½ cup milk

2 cups dry penne pasta

½ cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

Heat oil and add sliced polska kielbasa (in discs) and onion to cook for 3-5 minutes. Add veggies, broth, milk and pasta. Cover and cook until pasta is done, about 15 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese and salt and pepper.
