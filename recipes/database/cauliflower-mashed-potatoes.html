Cauliflower “Mashed Potatoes”

Ingredients

3 pounds cauliflower

½ teaspoon salt

1 Tablespoon butter or olive oil

Instructions

Break cauliflower into pieces and boil for 10 minutes or steam on high in the instant pot for 4 minutes. Let drain completely. Add butter or olive oil and salt and mash in a blender, food processor or with hand mixer if you like it chunkier.
