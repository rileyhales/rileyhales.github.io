Creamy Corn and Turkey Soup

Ingredients

½ cup chopped onion

2 Tablespoons butter or margarine

4 oz (half of an 8 oz package) cream cheese, cubed

1 can cream style corn

2 cups chicken broth

¾ cup milk

2 cups cut up turkey (or chicken) leftovers

Instructions

Cook onion in butter in saucepan until tender, stirring frequently. Add cream cheese and cook until melted. Add corn, broth, milk and turkey. Mix well. Cook until heated through.

Yield 6 - 1 cup servings
