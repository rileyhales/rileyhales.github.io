BBQ Pork Ribs (crockpot)

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds or 1 package pork loin ribs (I prefer the boneless ribs)
  • 1 onion, sliced and put in the bottom of the crockpot
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ¾ cup apple juice
  • Favorite BBQ sauce

    • Instructions

    Place onion in bottom of crockpot and place ribs on top. Add garlic, apple juice, and sprinkle brown sugar over the top. Cook about 4 hours on low. Remove ribs and empty the crockpot. Add 1 ½ cups of BBQ sauce to the top of ribs. Cook until warmed up to an hour more.
