Norskies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

    • Instructions

    Mix dry ingredients, egg and milk. Melt butter and add to the batter. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto a 9-inch frying pan lightly greased with butter. Spread evenly while twisting the pan. Remove from the pan when lightly brown on both sides.

    Yield: about 8 norskies.
