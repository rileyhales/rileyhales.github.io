Sunday Hostess Crumb Cake Copycat Muffins

Ingredients

1 ½ cups flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

⅛ teaspoon salt

½ cup butter

⅔ cup sugar

2 eggs

⅓ cup milk

½ teaspoon vanilla

Topping:

¼ cup brown sugar

¼ cup sugar

½ teaspoon cinnamon

⅓ cup melted butter

1 cup flour

Instructions

Combine flour, baking powder and salt in small bowl. Beat butter and sugar for 3 minutes. Add eggs and vanilla. Starting with a half of the flour mixture and ending with the last half of flour mixture, alternately add flour and milk to butter and sugar mixture. Use cookie scoop or ¼ cup measuring cup to fill muffin cups.

For the topping, mix sugars, cinnamon and flour together, Add in melted butter last and mix with fork or fingers to make a crumbly topping. If you mix too much you will end up with a dough and not crumbs. Sprinkle 1 heaping tablespoon onto the top of each muffin. Bake for 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes. Cook on rack and sprinkle with powdered sugar.

Yield:12 muffins
