Crockpot Chicken and Stuffing with Sauce

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 boxes of stove top stuffing
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 bag frozen green beans

    • Instructions

    This recipe can easily be halved if needed. Place chicken in the bottom of the crockpot. Spread stuffing mix on top and then a mixture of sour cream and cream of chicken soup. (I like to double the sauce part to make it saucier). Top with a bag of frozen green beans. Cook on low for 4 hours.
