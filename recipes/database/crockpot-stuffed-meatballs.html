Crockpot Stuffed Meatballs

Ingredients

1 pound hamburger

1 cup plain breadcrumbs

1 egg + 1 egg yolk

1 teaspoon minced garlic

1 teaspoon onion powder (or minced onion)

1 Tablespoon italian seasoning

1 teaspoon salt

½ teaspoon pepper

3-4 string cheese

Instructions

Cut cheese into thirds and put in the freezer. Mix remaining ingredients with hands. Scoop ¼ cup meat mixture and roll into a ball. Push 1 piece of cheese into the center. Put balls in crockpot and cook on high for 1-2 hours or on low for 3-4 hours.

*Meatballs can also be cooked in the oven at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
