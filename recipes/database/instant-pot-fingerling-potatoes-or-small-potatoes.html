Instant Pot Fingerling Potatoes or Small Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 1 ½ lbs. Fingerling potatoes or small potatoes, pricked with a knife or fork
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rosemary, dried or fresh

    • Instructions

    Preheat instant pot on saute. Add butter and once it is melted, add potatoes and stir to coat. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes or until the skins begin to be crispy and butter is brown, but not burned. Add broth. Cook on Manual, high pressure for 7 minutes and then natural release for 10 minutes. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and rosemary.

    *This recipe is for potatoes no bigger than 1 inch thick.
