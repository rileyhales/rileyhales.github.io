Brown Rice with Black Beans and Lentils

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • ½ cup dry green lentils
  • ½ cup brown rice
  • 1 can black beans (drained)
  • ¼ cup salsa
  • 2 ¼ cups chicken broth

    • Instructions

    Saute garlic and onion for 2-3 minutes (if using dried onion and minced garlic from a jar, I just add it all into the pan and skip the sauteing). Add rice, lentils, black beans, salsa and broth. Cook with the lid on 30-35 minutes (sometimes 40 minutes) until all the liquid is gone. If the liquid is gone and the lentils and rice aren’t tender, add more broth and cook for a few more minutes.
